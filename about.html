About Our Practice

Leading nephrology care in Northeast Florida for over three decades, dedicated to excellence in kidney health and patient-centered treatment.

Our Story

Founded in 2006 by Dr. R. Glenn Davis, Dr. Louis F. Gregory, and Dr. Andrew Wasiluk, Nephrology Consultants of Jacksonville has been at the forefront of kidney care in Northeast Florida. With roots stretching back over three decades, our physicians were pioneers in bringing dialysis services to Jacksonville.

In 2011, the group partnered with Dialysis Clinic, Inc. (DCI), one of the nation's largest nonprofit dialysis providers, enhancing our reach and quality of care. Today, we continue to deliver comprehensive nephrology services, from chronic kidney disease management to advanced kidney care facilities (KCF) and dialysis treatments across the region.
