Our Services

Comprehensive nephrology services designed to address all aspects of kidney health, from prevention to advanced treatment options.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Comprehensive management and treatment of chronic kidney disease with personalized care plans to slow progression and maintain quality of life.

  • Early detection and staging
  • Disease progression monitoring
  • Lifestyle and dietary counseling
  • Medication management

Dialysis Services

State-of-the-art dialysis facilities with experienced medical staff providing comfortable, safe, and effective treatment options.

  • In-center hemodialysis
  • Peritoneal dialysis
  • Home dialysis training
  • Vascular access care

Hypertension Management

Specialized treatment for kidney-related high blood pressure and cardiovascular complications with comprehensive monitoring.

  • 24-hour blood pressure monitoring
  • Medication optimization
  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Lifestyle modification plans

Kidney Transplant Care

Comprehensive pre and post-transplant care with thorough evaluation, preparation, and ongoing monitoring for optimal outcomes.

  • Pre-transplant evaluation
  • Post-operative care
  • Immunosuppression management
  • Long-term follow-up

Chronic Care Management

Ongoing support for patients with multiple chronic conditions, enhancing health outcomes and coordinating comprehensive care services.

  • Care plan development
  • Monthly check-ins
  • Medication reconciliation
  • Coordination of services

Transitional Care Management

Assisting patients during transitions from hospital to home to reduce readmission risks and ensure seamless continuity of care.

  • Discharge planning
  • Medication review
  • Post-discharge follow-up
  • Patient education

Principal Care Management

Focused management for patients with a single high-risk chronic condition, ensuring specialized care and improved health outcomes.

  • Individualized care planning
  • Regular disease monitoring
  • Medication management
  • Patient engagement

Kidney Biopsy

Advanced diagnostic procedures to determine the cause and extent of kidney disease using minimally invasive techniques.

  • Minimally invasive procedure
  • Expert pathological analysis
  • Rapid results and reporting
  • Treatment planning guidance

Family Education

Comprehensive education programs for patients and families about kidney health, treatment options, and lifestyle management.

  • Nutritional counseling
  • Lifestyle modification guidance
  • Support group facilitation
  • Educational workshops
